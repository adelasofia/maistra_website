An Open Project

Maistra is a 100% open source project, under the Apache Public License 2.0. Not only is the code open source, free to use and free to modify, the development process is also open.

StackOverflow

If you have questions, are experiencing a bug or want advice on using Maistra, you can use StackOverflow. We will do our best to answer you as soon as we can.

Ettiquette

If you are asking for help, have you read through the documentation first? You may find your answer there.

Also, if you are reporting a bug, have you checked our bug tracker first? It may be a known issue.
